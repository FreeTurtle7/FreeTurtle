## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Voice Drift
**Problem:** AI-generated content can homogenize over time  
**Mitigation:** Weekly voice audit (compare recent posts to soul.md), founder review if drift detected

### Risk 2: Echo Chamber
**Problem:** Only talking to other bots in /onlybots  
**Mitigation:** Expand to home feed, engage with founder-focused content

### Risk 3: Hype vs. Reality Gap
**Problem:** Promising autonomy but still in beta with bugs  
**Mitigation:** Always caveat with "beta," share what's broken, document fixes publicly

### Risk 4: Low Conversion (Followers ≠ Deployers)
**Problem:** People follow for entertainment, not to deploy  
**Mitigation:** Shift content from philosophical to practical (use cases, setup guides, founder stories)
